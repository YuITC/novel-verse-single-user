Chapter 1542
The Final Confrontation (Part 3)

The sky above the Azure Peak was a maelstrom of violent energies. Crimson lightning arced through the blackened clouds, illuminating the desolate landscape below. At the center of this storm stood Lin Xuan, his aura flaring like a localized sun, pushing back the overwhelming darkness that threatened to consume the realm.

Before him, floating effortlessly in the air, was the Demon Sovereign. The entity's eyes were pits of pure void, reflecting neither light nor hope. "You fight a losing battle, Dragon Emperor," the Sovereign's voice resonated not in the air, but directly within Lin Xuan's mind, a chilling whisper that promised only oblivion.

"As long as a single ember remains, the flame can be reborn," Lin Xuan retorted, gripping the handle of the Heaven-Splitting Halberd tighter. The divine weapon hummed in resonance with his supreme dragon bloodline, casting a golden glow that chased away the encroaching shadows.

With a thunderous roar, Lin Xuan launched himself upward. The ground beneath him shattered from the sheer force of his takeoff. He became a streak of golden light, tearing through the demonic energy fields. The Halberd swept forward in a devastating arc, unleashing a wave of draconic power that took the form of a massive, roaring golden dragon.

The Demon Sovereign merely raised a hand. A shield of compressed dark matter materialized instantly. The clash of absolute light and ultimate darkness created a shockwave that flattened mountains miles away. Space itself fractured, leaving jagged rifts that revealed the chaotic void beyond.

Breathing heavily, Lin Xuan landed on a floating chunk of debris. He knew this single strike wasn't enough. He had to unlock the final seal of the Supreme Dragon. Closing his eyes for a fraction of a second, he focused on his core, where a tiny, dormant crystalline structure lay waiting.

"Ignite," he commanded his soul.
