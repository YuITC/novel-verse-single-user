Qi Condensation

Meridian Flow Diagram

Detailed Description

Qi Condensation is the first step on the path to immortality. Mortals must first sense the spiritual energy of heaven and earth, then draw it into their bodies to open their meridians. This process cleanses the mortal body of impurities, granting increased strength, vitality, and minor disease resistance.

It is divided into 9 distinct levels. Levels 1-3 involve sensing and gathering Qi. Levels 4-6 focus on opening the minor meridians. Levels 7-9 require connecting the major meridians to form a complete circuit leading to the dantian.
